Adobe Research — Internal Report, 2025

Token-Efficient Communication Protocols for Multi-Agent LLM Systems

Venkatesh Akula, Adobe Research

Proposed and evaluated communication protocols that reduce inter-agent token overhead by ~35% while maintaining or improving task accuracy on HotpotQA, GPQA, and GSM8K benchmarks. Compared AutoGen, LangChain, DSPy, and TextGrad frameworks.
